About Us

From Felix Gan (CEO, Photography Xchange)

As the founder of photography xchange, I feel obligated to talk about my childhood hardships. There once was a poor family who worked tirelessly to earn enough money to buy a camera. The parents worked long hours at grueling jobs, while the children did their part by taking on odd jobs and chores to contribute to the family's income. Despite the challenges they faced, the family remained determined and focused on their goal. Eventually, after months of hard work and sacrifice, the family was able to save up enough money to purchase the camera they had been dreaming of.

But the path to photography should not be obstructed by wealth. Photography Exchange seeks to provide a marketplace where people can exchange high-quality camera gear at affordable prices.
